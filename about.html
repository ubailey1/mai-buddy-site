About MAI Buddy

MAI Buddy was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves a safe space to reflect and grow. In a world that's always moving, taking a moment for yourself is more important than ever. We wanted to create a companion that's always there to listen, without judgment, and to help you navigate the ups and downs of life.

Our Mission: Our mission is to make mental wellness accessible to everyone. We believe that by providing personalized, AI-powered support, we can help people build healthier mental habits, gain clarity, and find a sense of calm in their daily lives.

We are a small team of developers, designers, and mental health advocates dedicated to creating a tool that is not only effective but also private and secure. Your journey is your own, and we're just here to provide a helping hand along the way.
